Other accepted open licenses include:
- **UK Open Government License** — For UK government data
- **Apache 2.0** — For software and data
- **MIT License** — Simple permissive license

If your data uses a different open license, please ensure it meets the [Open Definition](https://opendefinition.org/) criteria.
