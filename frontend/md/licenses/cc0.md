---

CC0 is the "no copyright reserved" option:
- **No requirements** for attribution
- **Maximum freedom** for data users
- Data is essentially in the **public domain**

**Best for:** Data where you want to waive all rights and impose zero restrictions

**License URL:** [https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)
