Carly has 25 years of experience in project and programme delivery, with the last 18 years dedicated to sustainable mobility. Prior to becoming the Secretary General of SLOCAT, she was the Team Leader for Active Mobility and for Transport Digitalisation and Modal Integration at the UNEP Sustainable Mobility Unit, headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. Carly’s Active Mobility portfolio focused on promoting walking and cycling and access to public transport as central to sustainable, low-carbon transport systems. She spearheaded the development of the first ever Pan African Action Plan for Active Mobility (PAAPAM) which has been co-adopted by WHO, UN-Habitat, African Union and UNECE.
