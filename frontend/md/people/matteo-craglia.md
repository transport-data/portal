Matteo’s work focusses on energy technologies and the transport sector. At UC Davis he leads the development of the transport sector energy model. Prior to joining UC Davis, Matteo worked at the International Transport Forum and the International Energy Agency on topics including energy efficiency and transport. Matteo holds a PhD in Engineering from the University of Cambridge.
